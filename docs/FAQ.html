Frequently Asked Questions


What does Ethos Consulting do?

We provide businesses with business strategies, data privatization, and sex and race equity to build more ethical business practices.

What industries do you work within?

We work with companies from various industries. Our target market is large businesses and corporations that are currently using outdated and unethical business strategies.

Where are you located and how do you work with clients outside of your local area?

We are based in New York City. For clients outside of our local area, depending on the project we will either assist them virtually or we will have consultants visit them. We will also create a website that allows clients to log in and gain access to their analytics, and progress for their company. Consultants will update the website when there is a new advancement the clients should know about.

What are your consulting rates?

Pricing will be determined by the hours our team spends on your case, a base amount.
Base amount will be $300
Hourly rate is $85

How long does it take?

It depends on the project and the size of the company.

How will the consulting process work?

Clients will be provided a team of consultants who will gain knowledge of the company’s current business strategies, programs, finances, and goals for the future of their company. Using all this information, the team will then devise a plan to aid the business in attaining their goal.
