ABOUT US


We are a midsized consultancy firm specializing in incorporating ethical business practices into the daily decisions of companies.

Our Mission

We at Ethos Consulting firm believe that every decision you make should be an ethical one. That’s why we make it our main purpose to aid businesses to build more ethical practices to better their company and their community.

How Ethos Was Founded

Ethos is an ethical business consulting firm founded in January 2021. Ethos was created to tackle the social injustice in professional workplaces on the rise and to help businesses make ethical decisions that will be better for everybody.

What Makes Ethos Unique

Along with advising our clients on how to build better practices, we are actively working on making our workplace an inclusive and socially responsible space. We are practicing what we preach!
